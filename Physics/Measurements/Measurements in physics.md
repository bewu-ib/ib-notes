# Measurements in Physics

7 **fundamental units**:
- m - *distance*
- kg - *mass*
- s - *time*
- A - *electric current*
- K - *temperature*
- cd (candela) - *luminous intensity*
- mol - *amount of substance* - Avogadro's constant = $6.02*10^{23}$ 

Other units:
- N - *force* = $kgms^{-2}$
- J - *energy* = $Nm = kgm^2s^{-2}$
- W - *power* = $Js^{-1} = kgm^2s^{-3}$ 
- C (coulomb) - *electric charge* = $As$
